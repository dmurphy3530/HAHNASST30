Find-All Form

This form displays all the symptoms which contain your word(s); you can select symptoms from this list.

Both the “Find” and “Find All” forms have "Select" and "Must Use" buttons and a "Weight" box just like the Symptom List on the Main Form. You may select symptoms from the “Find” or “Find All” lists the same way you do with the Main Form Symptom List. When you are finished selecting from the list, click the "Done" button at the bottom to pop it down.
